Device Repair Services & Diagnostics

iMak Repairs provides professional diagnostic assessments, general repair services and compatible replacement options for phones, laptops, tablets and gaming consoles. All repairs are performed as an independent service provider, and we are not affiliated with or authorized by any device manufacturer or brand.

Our Repair Service Categories

Our technicians perform diagnostics first to help identify the condition of the device before repair. Parts used may vary based on availability and customer preference, and repair results may depend on the condition of the device at the time of service.

Phone & Tablet Repair

Screen damage, battery replacement, charging issues, button faults, speaker or microphone issues, software troubleshooting and diagnostics.

Laptop & Computer Repair

Hardware diagnostics, performance issues, storage upgrades, keyboard and cooling service, system troubleshooting and common repair services.

Game Console Repair

Performance and overheating diagnostics, startup issues, controller repair, port and connection issues, and general console servicing.

Accessory Support & Replacement

Assistance with compatible cables, chargers, headphones and general device accessory support based on availability and suitability.

Service Approach & Transparency

Each device is assessed individually. Repair eligibility, turnaround time and cost may vary depending on the condition of the device and parts availability. Customers are informed of service options before any work is completed.
