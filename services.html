In-Store Device Repairs

We provide in-store hardware repair services for phones, tablets, laptops, and game consoles. We inspect the device and explain repair options and pricing before any work is done. Parts availability may vary by model.

Repair Services

Our services focus on physical hardware repairs performed in our Orangeville shop. If a repair is not possible due to device condition or parts availability, we will let you know before proceeding.

Phone & Tablet Repairs

Screen replacement, battery replacement, charging port repair, button repair, speaker and microphone repair, and other hardware repairs depending on the device model.

Laptop & Computer Repairs

Laptop screen replacement, battery replacement, keyboard/trackpad repair, fan and cooling repairs, and other in-store hardware repairs based on the device model.

Game Console Repairs

HDMI port repair, controller repair, charging and port repairs, and other console hardware repairs depending on device condition and parts availability.

Accessories

Compatible cables, chargers, cases, screen protectors, and other accessories for phones, tablets, laptops, and game consoles (availability may vary).

Repair Process & Transparency

We review the device in-store and confirm repair options, estimated turnaround time, and pricing before starting. Repairs and parts availability depend on the specific model and the condition of the device.
